Welcome to MangoSynth24!
This site uses the Web Audio API to generate sounds.
To use this site, you must first allow the browser to use the Web Audio API.

Controls

Knobs

  • Click and drag on the knobs to adjust the values.
  • You can also scroll the mousewheel while hovering over a knob.
  • Hold SHIFT on the keyboard while adjusting knobs to fine-tune.
  • Hold CTRL on the keyboard and click a knob to return it to default.
  • Right-click a knob to bring up the MIDI-learn context menu.
Note: Changing octave/detune while holding a key will cause it to stick down! This is a bug.
To fix, either switch back to the octave/detune value you were on and press the same key...
Or reload the page. I apologize for this, it's a tricky bug to squash üêõ

Keyboard

  • Click the keys on the keyboard to play a sound.
  • You can also use your computer keyboard if your cursor is hovering over it.
  • ZXC-row = white keys, first octave
  • ASD-row = black keys, first octave
  • QWE-row = white keys, second octave
  • Num-row = black keys, second octave

Switches

  • Click on the green buttons to turn parts of the synth on and off.

Selectors

  • Click on the select boxes in LFO & FX to change modulation target & effect type
Note: LFO select is currently broken, but FX select works!
